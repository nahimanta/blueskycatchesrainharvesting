We may collect personal information that can identify you, including but not limited to:

  • Property details for rainwater harvesting system design and installation
  • Payment information for service transactions
  • Government-issued identification when required for permits or rebates

2.2 Non-Personal Information

We also collect non-personal information that does not directly identify you, including:
